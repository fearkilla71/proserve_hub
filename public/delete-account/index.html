Delete Your Account

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your ProServe Hub account and associated personal data.

How to request account deletion

  • In the app: Sign in, open account settings, and select Delete Account.
  • By email: Send a request to support@proservehub.app with subject line Account Deletion Request.

Important: We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before processing a deletion request.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile information (name, email, phone, ZIP, role)
  • Saved profile details and preferences
  • Messages and related in-app communication records
  • Associated user-generated account data that is not legally required to retain

What data may be retained

  • Transaction or payout records required for legal, tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention obligations
  • Security logs required to prevent abuse and protect platform integrity

Deletion timeline

Once we verify your request, account deletion is processed within 30 days.

Need help?

If you need help with account deletion, contact us:

Email: support@proservehub.app
Website: proservehub.app
