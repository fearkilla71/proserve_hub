Frequently asked questions.

Is ProServe Hub really free to start?▼
Yes! The Basic plan is completely free, forever. You get job management, client messaging, a proof feed, and up to 3 active jobs. Upgrade to Pro or Enterprise when you're ready for AI tools and unlimited jobs.
How does the escrow payment system work?▼
When a customer books a job, their payment is held securely in escrow via Stripe. Once the job is completed and both parties confirm, funds are released to the contractor. This protects both sides and eliminates payment chasing.
Do I need to be tech-savvy to use this?▼
Not at all. ProServe Hub is designed for contractors, not developers. The AI handles the heavy lifting — just snap photos, confirm updates, and let the app do the rest. If you can use Instagram, you can use ProServe Hub.
What types of contractors is this for?▼
Any home service professional — painters, plumbers, electricians, roofers, landscapers, general contractors, HVAC techs, and more. If you do jobs for clients, ProServe Hub works for you.
How does the AI generate estimates?▼
You provide the job scope (description, photos, area) and our AI analyzes it against market rates, material costs, and your pricing history to draft a professional estimate. You review and tweak before sending to the client.
Can I try it before committing?▼
Absolutely. The Basic plan is free with no credit card required. You can explore the full interface, manage jobs, and see how the platform works before deciding to upgrade.
Is my data secure?▼
Yes. We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for infrastructure, Stripe for payments, and follow industry best practices for data encryption. Your business data is yours — we never sell it or share it with third parties.
When does ProServe Hub launch?▼
We're in the final stages of development and will be launching on both Google Play and the App Store very soon. Join the waitlist to get early access and be the first to know when we go live!
