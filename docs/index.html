Frequently asked questions.

Is ProServe Hub free for homeowners?▼
Yes! Homeowners use ProServe Hub completely free — forever. Get instant AI estimates, compare contractor bids, track your project, and pay securely through escrow at no cost to you.
How does the AI estimate work?▼
Describe your project (and add photos if you want). Our AI analyzes the scope, materials, and local market rates to generate a detailed line-by-line estimate in seconds. You can lock that price for 24 hours and book a contractor at that rate, or open it up for pros to submit custom bids.
What does "24-hour price lock" mean?▼
When AI generates an estimate, you can lock that price for 24 hours. If you book a contractor within that window, the AI price is guaranteed. After 24 hours, you can generate a new estimate or accept contractor bids at their quoted prices.
How does escrow protect me as a homeowner?▼
When you book and pay, your funds are held securely in escrow via Stripe. The contractor can see the funds are committed, but they're not released until the job is complete and you approve the work. If there's a dispute, the funds stay locked while we help resolve it.
Will I get spam calls like on Thumbtack?▼
Absolutely not. On ProServe Hub, contractors bid on your project inside the app. You choose who to contact. Your phone number is never shared until you explicitly decide to connect with a contractor.
Is it free for contractors to start?▼
Yes. The Basic plan is free forever — manage up to 3 active jobs, message clients, and build your proof feed. Upgrade to Pro ($11.99/mo) for AI estimates, unlimited jobs, escrow payments, and priority lead matching.
What types of projects does ProServe Hub support?▼
Any home service — kitchen remodels, bathroom renovations, painting, roofing, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, fencing, HVAC, flooring, and more. If it's a project around your home, ProServe Hub can help you find and manage the right pro.
